Global Game Jam Berlin 2015 @ Wooga

Global Game Jam is an international hands-on Game Festival which takes place in over 450 cities world wide on just one weekend. The participants will produce experimental computer games which will be uploaded to the main GGJ website at the end of the weekend. From there everyone can download and play the games.

Global Game Jam is a wonderful opportunity for participants to expand their horizons, challenge themselves, and meet new people. Right away you will start developing a playable game with strangers who share the same passion as you in just two days. At the end you are tired but happy.

Global Game Jam Berlin is open to everyone who wants to create digital games, board games or physical games in 48 hours.

The 2015 edition will take place at the Wooga office on the weekend 23-25 of January.

Information

Who

The Global Game Jam Berlin is open to everyone.
There is no special skillset required, all disciplines are welcome.
Unter 18 must be accompained by an adult.

What

Any kind of game: digital, non-digital, physical...
Any kind of platform: desktop, web, mobile, pen-and-paper...

When

January 23, 2015 to January 25, 2015.
Starts on Friday at 5pm and ends on Sunday at 4pm.
Presentation starts on Sunday at 5pm.

Where

Wooga offices at Die Backfabrik, 4th floor. Saarbrücker Straße 38, 10405 Berlin.

View Larger Map

Accommodation

The GGJ is a 48 hour event and will keep going overnight.
Unfortunately we cannot provide a special room to sleep in the venue.

Ownership

The rights of the game that you make will be owned by you. It is required that you upload it to the Global Game Jam website and released under a Creative Commons License, both source code and assets, including permission for Global Game Jam to use your content for promotion purposes.

Presentations

The teams will present their games in the auditorium on Sunday 25th of January at 17:00. The presentations are open to the general public (get free ticket here).
